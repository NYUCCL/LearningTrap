Beekeeping test phase

You will now be tested on how well you have learned to harvest honey from friendly hives while avoiding dangerous hives.

As before, you will visit each of the beehives and have to decide whether to harvest honey from it or avoid it. There have been no changes to which bee varieties are friendly and which are dangerous.

Just like before, collecting honey adds $0.02 to your bonus, getting stung subtracts $0.10, and avoiding a hive adds $0.00. However, in the test phase you will not receive any feedback. You won't be able to see whether the bees were friendly or dangerous, and you won't see any changes to your bonus.
