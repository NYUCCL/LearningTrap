Instructions

In this experiment, you will play a simple video game in which you take the role of a beekeeper collecting honey.

Most of your beehives have friendly bees, which will allow you to collect honey. Unfortunately, some of your colonies have been infiltrated by dangerous bees, which will sting you if you approach them. Your job is to collect as much honey as possible from friendly bees, while learning to avoid dangerous bees so you aren't stung.
