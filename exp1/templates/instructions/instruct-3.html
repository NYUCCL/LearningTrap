Instructions

Each hive is home to a single variety of bees, and these varieties have different features which can allow you to tell them apart and tell which will be dangerous.

The bee varieties differ in four ways.

They can have 2 or 6 legs ,

single or double wings,

stripes or dots on their body,

and antennae or no antennae.

It's possible to learn to predict perfectly which bees are friendly and which are dangerous using these four features, so that you can harvest from bees that will give you honey while avoiding bees that would sting you.
