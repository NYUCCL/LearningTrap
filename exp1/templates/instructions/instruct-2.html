Instructions

You will collect honey from beehives, which contain several different bee varieties. At each hive you will see one of the bees that live there and then need to choose whether to harvest honey from the hive or to avoid it.

If you choose to harvest the hive, you'll be able to collect honey if the bee variety is friendly, but will be stung if the bee variety is dangerous. Collecting honey adds $0.02 to your bonus for the experiment, while being stung subtracts $0.10 because you need expensive medicine to treat the sting.

You begin with a bonus of $0.40. There will be no changes in which bee varieties are friendly and which are dangerous over the course of the experiment.
