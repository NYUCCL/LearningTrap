Final applicant batch

You will now evaluate a final batch of applicants. Unlike before, you will have to make your decisions about whether to accept or reject all of the applicants before finding out which were suitable and which were unsuitable, and how your company's revenue has been affected.

Just as before, accepting a suitable applicant will increase revenue by 1 thousand dollars, and accepting an unsuitable applicant will decrease revenue by 3 thousand dollars. Your goal is still to finish the experiment with high revenue. However, you will not be able to view changes in revenue or your company's total revenue until the end of this final batch. There have been no changes in the attributes that indicate a suitable or unsuitable applicant.
