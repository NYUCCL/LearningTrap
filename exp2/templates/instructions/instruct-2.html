Instructions

In this experiment, you will play a simple game in which you are a recruiter considering job applicants. Your goal is to create revenue for your company.

You will see 128 applicants, and must choose whether to accept or reject each one. Some applicants are suitable to work at your company, while some are unsuitable.

If you choose to accept an applicant, you'll learn whether they are suitable or unsuitable. If they are suitable, your company's revenue will increase by 1 thousand dollars. However, if they are unsuitable, your company's revenue will decrease by 3 thousand dollars.

Your company begins with 50 thousand dollars of revenue. At the end of the experiment, you will receive a bonus of 1 cent for each thousand dollars of revenue your company has.
